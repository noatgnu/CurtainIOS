# Privacy Policy for Curtain iOS

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Curtain is a proteomics data visualization application developed for the scientific research community with the aim to make data exploration sessions created from the main Curtain web application https://curtain.proteo.info available on iOS and macOS devices. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## 2. Information We Collect

Curtain iOS does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data processing happens locally on your device.

## 3. Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Proteomics Datasets:** Downloaded from curtain.proteo.info and cached locally for offline access
- **User Preferences:** App theme settings and display preferences
- **Analysis State:** Your selections, annotations, and plot customizations
- **Database Cache:** Local SQLite databases for performance optimization

**All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers except when downloading datasets from curtain.proteo.info.**

## 4. Network Access

The app requires internet access for:

- Downloading proteomics datasets from curtain.proteo.info

**No analytics, tracking, or user data is transmitted.**

## 5. External Connections

The app connects to **curtain.proteo.info**, which is maintained by the same developer, solely for downloading proteomics datasets. No user data is sent to any third-party services.

## 6. Data Security

- All data is stored locally using iOS secure storage mechanisms
- No user accounts or authentication required
- No cloud backup of sensitive research data
- Data is only accessible within the app's sandbox

## 7. Children's Privacy

Curtain is designed for exploring publicly available scientific datasets stored locally on the device. We do not collect any information from children.

## 8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Access:** All data is stored locally and accessible to you
- **Deletion:** Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
- **Data Export:** You can clear cached datasets via the app settings

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app and on this page. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent changes.

## 10. Open Source

Curtain iOS is open source software. You can review the complete source code to verify our privacy claims.

## 11. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how the app handles data, please contact:

- **Project Website:** [curtain.proteo.info](https://curtain.proteo.info)
- **GitHub:** Check the app's repository for issue reporting

## 12. Consent

By using Curtain iOS, you consent to this privacy policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.
